Lasagna


Description

A delicious Italian dish consisting of flat sheets of pasta that form layers.

Each layer can contain various ingredients such as ground meat, vegetables,

tomato sauce and cheese.


Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Beef
  • Mozzarella
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce and cheese
  3. Layer and bake
  4. Let the lasagna rest
